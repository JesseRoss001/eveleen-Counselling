Privacy & Data Policy - Eveleen Power Counselling

About Eveleen Power Counselling

Eveleen Power Counselling is a UK-based therapy and counselling service, fully insured to offer professional therapy and counselling sessions. We prioritise your privacy and ensure that all interactions and data are handled with the utmost care and confidentiality.

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. All client data is held securely and is only accessible by authorised personnel. We periodically delete data that is no longer required to ensure that we only retain information that is necessary for the provision of our services.

All data is stored in compliance with GDPR regulations, ensuring that your personal information is protected at all times.

Video Platform Compliance

Our sessions are hosted via a video platform that is HIPAA-approved, ensuring that all communications during your therapy sessions are secure and confidential. This platform complies with the highest standards of security to protect your privacy.

Insurance

Eveleen Power Counselling is fully insured, providing peace of mind that all services are covered by comprehensive professional liability insurance. This insurance is in place to protect both the client and the practitioner, ensuring a safe and secure environment for therapy.

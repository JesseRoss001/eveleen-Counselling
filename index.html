Therapies Offered

Behavioural Therapy

Empowers clients to find their own solutions in a supportive environment.

Integrative Counselling

Combines different therapeutic approaches to suit your individual needs.

Walk and Talk Therapy

Sessions conducted outdoors, combining physical movement with emotional exploration.

Somatic Therapy

Focuses on the connection between mind and body to address psychological and physical symptoms.
